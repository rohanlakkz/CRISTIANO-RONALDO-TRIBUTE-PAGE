Cristiano Ronaldo

  • 1985: Born on 5th Feb in Portugal
  • 1992: Joins neighborhood club Andorinha
  • 1995: Joins Nacional
  • 1997: Joins Sporting after trial, outshines everyone
  • 2002: Moves to Sporting B team
  • 2003: Impresses Manchester United in a friendly, joins them
  • 2004: Scores first Premier League goal vs Portsmouth
  • 2006: Wins 1st Premier League title with 20+ goals
  • 2007: Wins 2nd PL title, finishes 3rd in Ballon d'Or
  • 2008: Scores first PL hattrick, wins Ballon d'Or & Champions League
  • 2009: Transfers to Real Madrid
  • 2011: Wins La Liga with Real Madrid
  • 2014: Wins 2nd UCL title and Ballon d'Or
  • 2016: Wins 3rd UCL, Ballon d'Or, and Euro Cup with Portugal
  • 2017: Wins 4th UCL, Ballon d'Or, and La Liga
  • 2018: Wins 5th and final UCL title
  • 2019: Joins Juventus, wins Serie A and Nations League
  • 2020: Wins Serie A and Italian Cup
  • 2021: Returns to Manchester United
  • 2022: Joins Al-Nassr (Saudi Arabia)
